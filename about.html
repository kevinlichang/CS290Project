About Me

Just a guy who loves taking photos

Hello! I am Kevin Chang and the owner of Demo Photography Studios. I am an award-winning photographer and have done several shoots across the globe. But I am just someone who loves taking a perfect shot of the world and bringing it back for everyone to see!

I love traveling! Being able to visit so many different locations, photography is just a great way to share my experiences. As they say, a picture is worth a thousand words!
